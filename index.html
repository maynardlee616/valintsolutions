Guiding the Commercial Industry Through the Complexities of Government Contracting

As a government contractor, your company faces expanding complexities and oversight in winning and performing on federal contracts. As you seek to optimize financial performance and profitable growth, it’s critical to have a reliable and consultative advisor who understands your industry, provides customized solutions, and helps guide you forward.

Valint Solutions’s team of seasoned and experienced government contracting consultants is comprised of former military contracting and project management professionals. We are dedicated to mitigating government contract compliance risk and maximizing contract profitability, so you can focus on growing your business.

Federal contractors face increasing compliance regulations and audit requirements, including:

  • Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)
  • Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS)
  • Cost Accounting Standards (CAS)
  • Labor and employment rules
  • Increased emphasis on business systems and cybersecurity

We support all business types; whether you're a small growing business or an 8(a), SDVOB, VOSB, SDB, WOSB, or HUBZone company, Valint Solutions can help you make the most of emerging opportunities while minimizing FAR, DFARS, DCAA, and other government contract compliance issues. We support agencies such as DOD, VA, DHS, GSA, HHS, SBA, NASA, NIH, DOE, and USAID.
